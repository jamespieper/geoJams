Welcome to geoJams

The goal of this project is to find insights into the listening habits of various countries and cultures, and to help develop new perspectives on the way humans listen to music. Learn more in our process book.

The visulalization contain a few parts: in the center, you'll find a world map. The countries that are colored are the ones that we have data for. When you click on a country, you'll be able to see the top 3 trending songs in the country as well as some summary statistics on the left. Click on the ⓘ button next to each statistic to learn more about them. By default the map is colored by the average energy of the country's top 50 songs, but you can change it do be colored by the average danceability as well.
